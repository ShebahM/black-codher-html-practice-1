All About Matooke

Matooke is a banana native to East Africa. It's consistency is similar to mashed potatoes, as it is boiled and mashed.

How Matooke is cooked

Here are some facts about Matooke:

  1. Matooke is difficult to peel, while bananas peel very easily
  2. It is easily mistaken for green bananas
  3. They are short and chunky (in comparison to plantain)
